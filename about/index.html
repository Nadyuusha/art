About

This gallery is a quiet archive of paintings by a single artist. The work focuses on light, negative space, and slow observation.

Practice

  • Medium: oil and pigment on linen
  • Process: layered glazes and restrained palette
  • Themes: stillness, domestic light, and weathered surfaces

Statement

I paint to slow time. Each canvas is a study in restraint — a small room where color can breathe.

Exhibitions

Selected works are released in small series and documented here as they are completed.
